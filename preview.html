"My Time Machine"

Nov 15, 2018

Weight: 12.6 KB

History
Originally, I created this for my wife to help her keep track of the time she spent working on each job she was assigned because the company she works at bill their clients by the hour.

It began as a very basic blank time sheet that could be printed, and then filled in by hand. Evolving from that simple time sheet, this latest concept allows the user to create or delete, a table or rows with a few button clicks or finger taps.

To edit a table's "Header" or "Descriptions" double click on the cell. To insert a row under an existing row, double click cells in the "Hrs" column.

Unchecking the Table Name row removes the table (same for rows). To save: print the tables as a PDF (not available on all Android devices).

0.1 hours = 5 minutes
Entries 2 minutes long or less = 0.0

Due to developement cycles the image may not be an exact representation of the current version.
